Music Island

Music Island is where my passion for rock finds its home, a haven where the beat of the drum and the wail of the guitar speak to my soul. It's a place where I lose myself in the raw emotion and the spirit of bands like Deftones, Radiohead, and The Smashing Pumpkins. On Music Island, I'm free to explore the depths of my creativity, strumming chords and belting out lyrics that resonate with the angst and energy of youth. It's more than just a place to listen to music; it's a refuge where I can express myself authentically as well as my love for the edgy, unconventional sounds of what I like. Music Island is filled with large instruments, sheet music, band posters, and even music notes everywhere. Loud speakers blasting the new hit song stuck in my head, which is currently “Loose Cannon” by Puzzle. Which you should listen to. Theory happens everywhere there, as theory is my favourite part of music. Robots running around finding out how notes manage to fit together perfectly.
